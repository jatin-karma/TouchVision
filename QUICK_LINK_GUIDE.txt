## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Use short URLs?** Google Drive links are long. Consider:
   - Using bit.ly or tinyurl to shorten
   - Or leave full link (both work fine)

2. **Sharing Settings Matter:**
   - Right-click link → Share settings
   - Set to "Viewer" (read-only) for datasets
   - Anyone with link can access

3. **Test Your Links:**
   - Click each link before submitting
   - Make sure they actually work
   - Verify they show the right content

4. **Keep Links Updated:**
   - If you update files on Drive, update the link too
   - Or keep same link and replace files in Drive
